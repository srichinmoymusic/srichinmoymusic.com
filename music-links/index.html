Sri Chinmoy: Official Home Page

Sri Chinmoy is a spiritual teacher who has tirelessly dedicated his life to the fulfilment of the unlimited potential of the human spirit and to the pursuit of world oneness. This uplifting site provides an overview of Sri Chinmoy’s life and teachings, as well as his writings, music and artistic creations.

Sri Chinmoy Library

In over 1600 books of poetry, essays, plays and short stories, Sri Chinmoy’s words have conveyed the richness and diversity of the human quest for inner harmony and self-understanding. More than a thousand of Sri Chinmoy ’s inspiring works are online and searchable at this site.

Sri Chinmoy Songs

Sri Chinmoy has composed over 22,000 songs. These songs are primarily devotional in nature. Many are composed in his native Bengali tongue with about 8,000 composed in English. Sri Chinmoy Songs is a database containing many of these songs with a song sheet and words to the songs.

Radio Sri Chinmoy

Experience the meditation music of Maestro Sri Chinmoy as he sings and plays a dozen instruments from around the world.

Sri Chinmoy Art

Enter into an ever-glowing world of Sri Chinmoy’s vast meditative art works, which number over 140,000. View a selected on-line gallery of his “Fountain-Art”, with their brilliant colours and harmonious designs. Soon to come are selections from his 16 million soul-bird drawings.
